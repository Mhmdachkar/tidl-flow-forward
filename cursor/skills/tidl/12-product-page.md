# TIDL Product Detail Page (PDP) System

## Purpose

The Product Detail Page is the most important conversion page on the website.

Its purpose is not simply to display a product.

Its purpose is to:

* educate
* build trust
* answer objections
* explain the treatment
* explain the process
* guide users into the quiz

The page should feel like:

* a premium healthcare brand
* a medical consultation
* an educational experience

Not:

* an ecommerce store
* a supplement landing page
* Amazon
* Shopify templates

---

# Primary Goal

Move the visitor into:

```text
Start Assessment
Take Quiz
See If You Qualify
```

The product is prescribed.

The visitor is not purchasing immediately.

---

# Information Hierarchy

1. Outcome
2. Product
3. How it works
4. Why TIDL

* Product name

* Clinical background
* Floating particles

# Trust Bar

Immediately below hero:

* Licensed physicians
* Prescription treatment
* Delivered to your door
* Secure checkout
* HSA/FSA eligible

---

# Section 1: Outcomes

Headline:

```text
Designed for meaningful weight loss.
```

Metrics:

* average outcomes
* timeline
* expectations

Avoid promises.

Avoid guaranteed results.

Use disclaimers.

---

# Section 2: Why the Pen Matters

This is TIDL's biggest differentiator.

Explain:

* pre-dosed
* no mixing
* no measuring
* easier for beginners
* safer experience

Visual:

Large floating pen.

Exploded view if possible.

---

# Section 3: How It Works

5-step journey:

1. Take assessment
2. Doctor review
3. Prescription
4. Pharmacy ships
5. Ongoing care

This section is mandatory.

---

# Section 4: What's Included

Example:

* medication
* pen device
* instructions
* support
* physician review

Card layout.

---

# Section 5: Timeline

Example:

Week 1–4

Week 5–8

Week 9+

Educational.

Not:

90-day program.

Avoid fixed promises.

---

# Section 6: Safety

Must include:

* licensed doctors
* medical review
* eligibility requirements
* contraindications
* adult only

Medical questions go to physicians.

Never AI.

---

# Section 7: Reviews

Include:

* photos
* names
* outcomes
* stories

Social proof is critical.

---

# Section 8: FAQ

Examples:

* How does the pen work?
* Who qualifies?
* How long does shipping take?
* What happens after my quiz?
* Can I reorder?
* Is this prescription only?

---

# Final CTA

Large section:

```text
See if treatment is right for you.
```

CTA:

```text
Start Assessment
```
